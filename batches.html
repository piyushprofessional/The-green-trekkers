FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers for beginners before they book a trek.

What should I carry?

Carry trekking shoes, 2 litres of water, snacks, rainwear, torch, personal medicines and a small backpack.

Is food included?

Food depends on the trek package. You can confirm the exact inclusions on WhatsApp before payment.

Is transport included?

Pickup points are available only from Moshi and Chakan.

Can beginners join?

Yes, beginners can join easy and moderate treks. Difficult treks need basic stamina and prior trekking comfort.

What is the refund policy?

Refund and batch transfer rules depend on cancellation time. Contact support before cancelling. Read the full policy page for details.
