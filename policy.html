Terms & Conditions

Participant Consent & Trek Rules

  • Participants must follow trek leader instructions at all times.
  • Participants must disclose health issues, injuries, allergies or medication needs before the trek.
  • Participants must carry proper trekking shoes, water, torch, rainwear and personal medicines.
  • The organizer may change route, timing or cancel the trek due to weather, government restrictions or safety concerns.
  • No littering, alcohol, drugs, unsafe behaviour or damage to nature is allowed.
  • Booking is confirmed only after correct details, consent and payment/payment status are submitted.
  • Refunds and transfers follow the cancellation rules listed on this page.
