Cancellation, Refund & Safety Policy

Clear rules make your trekking website more professional and trusted.

✅

7+ Days Before Trek

Full batch transfer allowed or 80% refund after payment gateway / UPI checking.

⚠️

3 to 6 Days Before Trek

50% refund or one-time transfer to another available batch.

🚫

Less Than 72 Hours

No cash refund because transport, leader and food arrangements are already confirmed.

Weather Cancellation

If trek is cancelled by the team due to unsafe weather, users get batch transfer or refund as per organizer decision.
